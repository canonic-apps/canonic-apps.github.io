Part II: DETROSP

Seven dimensions of governance:

  • D — Declarative (inherits: MUST be absolute)
  • E — Evidentiary (timestamps MUST exist)
  • T — Taxonomic (series MUST match pattern)
  • R — Referential (VOCAB MUST define terms)
  • O — Operational (keywords MUST be normative)
  • S — Structural (closure MUST be complete)
  • P — Programming (code MUST follow LANGUAGE spec)
